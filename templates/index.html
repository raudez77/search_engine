Search By Keywords

Best Keyword Index

The broad search will help you capture and reduce time during searching, but it may also get you the most unnecessary SPO / Articles . Exact match search is much more restrictive, but it can also be very productive. You will get the SPO / Article when users query the exact keyword/phrase and nothing else. The down side Keyword searches may also fail to turn up related materials that don't specifically use the search term (false negatives).

Similar Meaning

Search by Meaning

An AI which is able to understand the meaning of the given word, and compare it with other articles, it will also show those SOP/Article related to the phrase that you might be using, is by far more powerful than seach by keywords only. The down side, it must be re-adjust from time to time but there are many tools able to solve this issue. Semantic search seeks to improve search accuracy by understanding the content of the search query. In contrast to traditional search engines which only find documents based on lexical matches, semantic search can also find synonyms
